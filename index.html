Our Story

From New York to Bhutan

It was my dream to open a NY pizzeria in Bhutan because where else is the best pizza in the world other than New York? After years of perfecting my craft in Brooklyn pizzerias, I brought the authentic taste back to my homeland.

At Karma's NY Pizza, we use only the finest ingredients imported directly from Italy and locally sourced fresh produce. Our dough is made fresh daily and hand-tossed to achieve that perfect thin crust New York is famous for.

Whether you're craving a whole pie or just a quick slice, we've got you covered with all the classic New York favorites and some unique Himalayan twists.
